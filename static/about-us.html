About Us

Hello fellow food lovers! It’s Alex and Kat here, the duo behind Food Inbox.

We’re globetrotting foodies, seeking out the best eats across Asia and beyond. Given the current events of the world, we’ve put our usual food adventures on hold - in the meantime, we’re exploring the flavours of our own city, Sydney.

While we enjoy the occasional fine dining experience, we’d much rather visit hole-in-the-wall joints and feast on no-frills food. Aesthetics are nice… but flavour is everything to us.

No matter what the meal, we’re always ready to write thoughtful, honest reviews about local Sydney restaurants and their food experience. We hope our content encourages readers to get out there and treat their tastebuds!
